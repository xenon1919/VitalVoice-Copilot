General Wellness Guide

1. Hydration:
Adults often benefit from regular water intake throughout the day.
Hydration needs vary with activity level, climate, and health conditions.

2. Sleep:
Consistent sleep schedules and adequate sleep duration support recovery and energy.

3. Headache and mild fatigue:
Potential contributors can include dehydration, stress, poor sleep, and prolonged screen exposure.
Supportive self-care can include hydration, rest, and reduced stress.

4. Red flag symptoms:
Chest pain, severe shortness of breath, sudden confusion, one-sided weakness, and fainting require urgent medical evaluation.
